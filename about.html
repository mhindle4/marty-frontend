About Marty

Marty is an AI chatbot trained on my own voice and personality. It can respond to text or voice messages and even speak back to you in my voice.

I created Marty to explore the future of conversational AI — blending natural dialogue with a personal touch. Whether you’re here to ask questions, hear a story, or just chat, Marty is ready to respond.

This project combines AI language models, voice synthesis, and a web-based interface so that anyone can connect and have a real-time conversation with my digital twin.
